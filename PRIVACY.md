# Privacy Policy for Sleeve

**Effective Date:** January 6, 2026

**1. Data Collection**
Sleeve does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to the developer or third parties. All data created within the app (Pockets, Items, Images, Notes) is stored locally on your device and synchronized securely via your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit technology.

**2. iCloud Sync**
Your data is stored in your private iCloud container. The developer of Sleeve does not have access to your data. Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy applies to the data stored in iCloud.

**3. Permissions**
- **Camera:** Used only to take photos for your pockets.
- **Photo Library:** Used only to select images for your pockets.
- **Location:** Used only to save location coordinates to your items or view them on a map. Location data is processed locally and stored in your private iCloud.

**4. Contact & Support**
If you have any questions, need support, or want to report a bug, please open a ticket on our support page:
https://github.com/kaasivik/Sleeve-info/issues
